# Fragility and External Dependence in Somalia

**Severity:** HIGH | **Type:** Security Threat | **Date:** 2025-01-01

> Serious situation requiring urgent attention from stakeholders.

## Alert Summary

- **Country:** Somalia
- **Region:** East Africa
- **Civic Space Status:** Repressed
- **Primary Dimension Affected:** D6 — Access to Funding

## Situation Description

Civic space under strain from fragility and external dependence.

## Dimension Context

This alert primarily concerns **Dimension 6: Access to Funding**. Funding and sustainability pressures threaten the long-term viability of independent observer organizations.

## Recommended Actions

### Short-term (1-4 weeks)

1. **Document** all incidents with dates, locations, and witnesses
2. **Report** to AfEONet regional coordinator and international partners
3. **Monitor** situation and prepare contingency plans
4. **Engage** diplomatic missions and international observer networks

### Medium-term (1-3 months)

1. **Advocate** with government authorities for observer protection
2. **Coordinate** with regional civil society networks
3. **Document** pattern of violations for formal reporting
4. **Seek** engagement from African Union and relevant regional bodies
