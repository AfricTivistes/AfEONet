# Police Raids on Observer Offices in Zimbabwe

**Severity:** CRITICAL | **Type:** Office Raid/Property Seizure | **Date:** 2025-02-20

> Immediate action required. Fundamental rights of observers are being violated.

## Alert Summary

- **Country:** Zimbabwe
- **Region:** Southern Africa
- **Civic Space Status:** Repressed
- **Primary Dimension Affected:** D4 — Security and Well-being
- **Observers Affected:** 15

## Situation Description

Police raided offices of the Zimbabwe Election Support Network. Equipment seized.

## Dimension Context

This alert primarily concerns **Dimension 4: Security and Well-being**. Restrictions on observer security and physical safety represent some of the most serious violations of civic space principles.

## Recommended Actions

### Immediate (0-72 hours)

1. **Document** all incidents with dates, locations, and witnesses
2. **Report** to AfEONet regional coordinator and international partners
3. **Suspend** field activities in high-risk areas pending security assessment
4. **Engage** diplomatic missions and international observer networks

### Short-term (1-4 weeks)

1. **Advocate** with government authorities for observer protection
2. **Coordinate** with regional civil society networks
3. **Document** pattern of violations for formal reporting
4. **Seek** engagement from African Union and relevant regional bodies
